80 Labs Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how 80 Labs collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our websites, tools, signal feeds, and related services.

Last updated: 30 November 2025
By using 80 Labs, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

In this Policy, “80 Labs,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to the owner of the 80 Labs services. “You” and “your” refer to any user or visitor of our services.

1. What this Privacy Policy covers

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle personal information when you:

  • Visit our websites, including 80labs.to and subpages such as /start;
  • Join our signal feeds, channels (for example Telegram), or communities;
  • Subscribe to our email lists or trading kits; or
  • Interact with any other products, tools, or services that link to this Policy

Collectively, we call these the “Services”.

2. Information we collect

We may collect three main types of information:

2.1 Information you provide directly

This includes information you choose to share with us, such as:

  • Contact details (for example, your email address when you request the trading kit or join a list);
  • Information you send us in messages, forms, or support requests;
  • Preferences or feedback you provide about the Services.

2.2 Information we collect automatically

When you use the Services, we may automatically collect certain technical information, such as:

  • Log data (for example, IP address, browser type, operating system);
  • Approximate location based on your IP address;
  • Pages you visit, links you click, and other usage data.

We may use cookies or similar technologies to remember settings and understand how people use the Services. You can usually adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but some features may not work properly.

2.3 Information from third parties

We may receive information about you from third‑party platforms when you interact with our presence there, for example:

  • When you join a Telegram channel we operate, Telegram may share your username or profile information with us according to their policies;
  • When you open our emails, our email service provider may record that the email was delivered or opened.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving the Services;
  • Sending you content you requested (for example, the trading kit, updates, or signals);
  • Communicating with you about changes, new features, or important notices;
  • Monitoring usage and trends to understand how the Services are used;
  • Protecting the security and integrity of the Services, and preventing abuse; and
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service.

4. Legal bases (if you are in the EU/UK or similar regions)

Where applicable law (for example, the EU/UK GDPR) requires a legal basis for processing personal data, we typically rely on:

  • Consent – for example, when you sign up to receive emails or marketing;
  • Legitimate interests – such as improving the Services, preventing abuse, or understanding usage, where these interests are not overridden by your rights;
  • Contract – where processing is necessary to provide Services you have requested; or
  • Legal obligations – where we are required to comply with applicable law.

You can withdraw consent at any time by using unsubscribe links in emails or by contacting us.

5. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information in the ordinary sense of the word. We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the Services (for example, email delivery providers, analytics tools, or hosting providers);
  • Professional advisors, such as lawyers or accountants, where necessary;
  • Authorities or law enforcement, when we reasonably believe disclosure is required by law or necessary to protect us, our users, or others; and
  • Successors in the event of a merger, acquisition, or other business transaction involving 80 Labs.

We require service providers to handle personal information in a manner consistent with this Policy and applicable law.

6. International data transfers

Because the internet is global, your information may be processed in countries other than the one where you live. These countries may have different data protection laws than your own.

Where required by law, we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers (for example, standard contractual clauses or similar mechanisms).

7. Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or allowed by law (for example, for tax, accounting, or legal reasons).

When we no longer need information, we will delete or anonymize it, or if that is not possible, securely store it and isolate it from further use.

8. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights in relation to your personal information, such as the right to:

  • Access a copy of the personal information we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information;
  • Request deletion of your information, in certain circumstances;
  • Object to or request restriction of certain processing;
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent; and
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

You can exercise these rights (where available) by contacting us at help@80labs.to. We may need to verify your identity before responding to certain requests.

9. Emails and marketing

If you subscribe to receive emails from us, we may send you updates, educational content, and news about 80 Labs. You can opt out at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us.

11. Children's privacy

The Services are not intended for children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

Your continued use of the Services after any changes means you accept the updated Policy. If you do not agree with the changes, you should stop using the Services.
